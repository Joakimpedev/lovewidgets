LoveWidgets - Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your account and all associated data, follow these steps:

Step 1: Open the LoveWidgets app on your device
Step 2: Tap on Settings (profile icon in the top corner)
Step 3: Navigate to Account settings
Step 4: Tap "Delete Account"
Step 5: Confirm deletion when prompted

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All drawings you've created
  • Partner connection and pairing data
  • App preferences and settings
  • Subscription information
  • Widget data

Need Help?

If you're unable to delete your account through the app, please contact us:

Email: support@lovewidgets.com

We'll process your deletion request within 30 days.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
